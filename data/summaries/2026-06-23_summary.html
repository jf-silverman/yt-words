🎙 Goldman Sachs: The Science of Portfolio Building [32:27]

Learning from wealthy individuals and Lee Cooperman taught humility, diversification, and how to match client temperament to portfolio construction.

  • Non-discretionary accounts forced him to sell ideas; learned to articulate thesis so clients understood and owned both upside and downside
