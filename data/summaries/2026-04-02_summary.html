AI platforms face same winner-take-all dynamics as late-1990s internet: only handful survive; infrastructure costs massive; losers disappear entirely.

  • Parallel to AI today: ChatGPT, Gemini, Meta AI, Copilot, Claude, Perplexity all seem like winners, but historical pattern suggests only handful survive.
  • Outcome mapping: one becomes America Online (near-total loss), one Infoseek (no future), one Yahoo (survives but loses relevance), one Lycos (disappears).
